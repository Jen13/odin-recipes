Making your own Hummus

Hummus is a versatile and tasty spread that's super easy to make! All you need is a can of chickpeas or garbanzo beans!

This simple recipe will guide you step by step through the process of making your very own hummus using common house hold ingredients.

Ingredients

  • chickpeas
  • garlic
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • water

steps

  1. Add chickpeas, garlic, salt and olive oil to a blender
  2. Blend the ingredientsuntil a paste starts to form
  3. Optionally add water a little at a time if you prefer a looser consistency
  4. Move blender contents to a dish and serve!
