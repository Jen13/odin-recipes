Roast Rabbit

Making garlic and herb roasted Rabbit

Tired of chicken? Try rabbit! Rabbit serves as a low cost white meat alternative to chicken.

This simple recipe will guide you step by step through the process of roasting a rabbit.

Ingredients

  • Rabbit
  • butter
  • salt
  • garlic
  • chives
  • oregano
  • parsley

steps

  1. in a bowl combine butter with minced garlic and herbs
  2. rub butter mixture around the surface of the rabbit
  3. Place rabbit in the center of an oven at 300 degrees for two hours
  4. Every fifteen minutes baste the rabbit with the melted butter and accumulated juices
  5. For the last fifteen minutes, set the oven to broil to brown the outer surface of the rabbit
  6. Allow the rabbit to rest for at least ten minutes befoe serving
